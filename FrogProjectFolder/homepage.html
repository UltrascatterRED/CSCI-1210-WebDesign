Mission Statement

This page is for frog fans of all kinds. Whether you are an avid frog enjoyer, or just have a passing interest in frogs, or even just like the color green, this page is for you! We personally love frogs, and want to be able to share that interest with you! This page aims to improve awareness of frogs and their species, as well as improve your personal knowledge of them.
