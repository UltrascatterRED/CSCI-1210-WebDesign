Lovely Poison Frog

The Lovely Poison frog is the smallest species in the Phyllobates genus. It only reaches two and a half (2.5) centimeters in length. They are primarily located in North America/Central America. They tend to be black with pale yellow to gold to orange stripes running down the body. The black is so deep they almost seem entirely black.
